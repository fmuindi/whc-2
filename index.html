Meet Our Pastor

Pastor Rod Parsley

Founder & Senior Pastor

Rod Parsley is one of America's foremost authorities on Christian social action, communicating the Gospel to millions worldwide through his television program Breakthrough.

For over four decades, Pastor Parsley has led World Harvest Church with a bold message of hope, healing, and revival — impacting generations both locally and globally.
